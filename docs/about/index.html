Our story

The Australian COVID-19 Modelling Initiative (ACMI) comprises several research groups, and aims to facilitate a rapid access to a collection of COVID-19 modelling studies, in an easy to find and understand format, for the public.

Our aims

  • Showcase modelling by independent academic groups, centres and institutes, across the country
  • Make COVID-19 modelling more transparent for the public
  • Compare modelling approaches and outcomes
  • Discuss emerging differences in modelling research and policy implications
